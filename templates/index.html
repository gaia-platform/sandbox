For the Gaia Sandbox system to identify who is sending in code, we store a single strictly necessary cookie on your browser which contains a randomly generated session ID. This ID does not contain you IP address or any personal information. Deleting this cookie will simply reset your Sandbox experience, which will mean you lose any code or edits made in the sandbox. Cookies will not be used for anything other than to help route sandbox commands to and from your computer.
